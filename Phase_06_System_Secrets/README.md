# 🔑 Phase 6: System Secrets (Security & Ownership)

### 🎯 What we are covering:
Who is the boss of this computer? We will learn:
* **The SuperUser:** Using `sudo` to get administrative power.
* **Permissions:** Understanding Read (`r`), Write (`w`), and Execute (`x`).
* **Links:** Creating "Shortcuts" for your files so they can be in two places at once.

### ❓ Why it matters:
Linux is built for multiple people to use at once. Understanding permissions is the most important part of "Cybersecurity." It ensures the right people have access and the wrong people (like hackers) stay out.
