# 📜 02. History of Linux

> **Goal:** Meet the creator and the famous penguin.

### 🕒 The Timeline
* **1991:** A 21-year-old student named **Linus Torvalds** from Finland created Linux as a hobby project.
* **The Goal:** He wanted a free version of the Unix system that could run on his own PC.
* **Collaboration:** He posted his code online and asked other programmers to help. This was the start of the **Global Linux Community**.

### 🐧 Meet Tux
* The official mascot of Linux is **Tux**, the penguin.
* **Tux** stands for **T**orvalds **U**ni**X**.
