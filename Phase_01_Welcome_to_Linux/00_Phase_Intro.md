# 🌟 Phase 1: Welcome to the World of Linux

### 🎯 What we are covering:
In this section, we take our first steps! We will learn:
* **The Origin Story:** Who created Linux and why it’s "Free and Open Source."
* **The Flavors:** Why there are so many versions (Distributions) like Ubuntu and Mint.
* **The Toolbox:** How to install new software using Package Managers.

### ❓ Why it matters:
Most of the world's smartest technology—from NASA’s Mars Rover to the servers that run YouTube and Instagram—runs on Linux. Understanding the "Why" and "How" of Linux gives you the keys to the most powerful operating system on the planet!
