# 🍦 05. Distributions of Linux (Distros)

> **Goal:** Understand the different "flavors" of Linux.

Since Linux is open source, anyone can package it with different tools. These versions are called **Distributions** (or **Distros**).

### 🏘️ Common Families:
* **Ubuntu:** The most popular for **beginners**. Very friendly and easy to use.
* **Linux Mint:** Perfect for people moving from **Windows**.
* **Fedora:** Always has the **newest** technology features.
* **Kali Linux:** Built specifically for **Cybersecurity** and hacking tests.
* **Debian:** Famous for being the most **stable** and reliable.
